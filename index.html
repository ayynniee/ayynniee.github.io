I am a twin from a family of five, part of this tightly-knit, large family tree branching all over the world; I grew up showered with love and fighting my siblings. I now live in New York City - still struggles sometimes with subway commutes from Manhattan to school in Brooklyn, but also loving the city more than ever. When I am not pulling off an all-nighter for projects, I am either adding to my city adventures, on my way to one, or yelping for my next.
